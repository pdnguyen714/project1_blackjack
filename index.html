Rules of the Game

Blackjack consists of two or more players. Each player is dealt two cards. Players each have the option to either "Hit" to draw a card, or "Stay" to keep the cards they have when it is their turn.

The goal is to get to 21 points. The winner is the person who is closest to 21 points. If both players go over 21 points, they both lose.

If one person goes over 21 and the other person is under 21, the person under 21 points wins.

Jack - Queen - King = 10pts
Ace = 1pt or 11pts - player's choice
